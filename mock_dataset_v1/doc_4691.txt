Question for written answer E-005192/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Niki Tzavela (EFD) (27 May 2011) Subject: Ebola virus in Uganda According to reports in the Greek press, initial test results indicate that the deadly Ebola virus has caused the death of a 12-year-old girl in a town 35 km north of Kampala (Uganda).The World Health Organisation's contagious disease and prevention officer in Uganda, Dr Miriam Nanyunja, said tests carried out at the Ugandan Virus Research Institute showed that the girl died of the virus on 6 May 2011 at Bombo hospital. In light of Dr Nanyunja's statement, in which she said that the health authorities are currently trying to establish whether there are any more cases in the area, could the Commission say how it might support the work being carried out by doctors in this region?
