IMCO ADR, ODR, Roaming and Polish Presidency 20.12.2011 - 15:00 - 18:30 - ASP5G3 Members received the presentation of the proposals and the impact assessments on Alternative Dispute Resolution (Louis GRECH (S&D, MT)) and Online Dispute Resolution (Róża Gräfin von THUN UND HOHENSTEIN (EPP, PL)) for consumer disputes. A debate on the roaming proposal was held and the Undersecretary of State Ms. Grażyna Henclewska discussed the conclusions of the Polish Presidency.
