Question for written answer E-9141/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Anna Rosbach (EFD) (8 November 2010) Subject: Recycling of paper in the Commission A large amount of paper is used in the EU institutions each year. How much of this comes from recycling, and how much is sent for recycling after use? 1. Approximately how much paper is used each year by the Commission (including in various publications)? 2. How much of this paper has been entirely or partly recycled? 3. How much paper does the Commission send for recycling each year? 4. Are there clauses in procurement agreements etc. on how much paper should come from recycled material? 5. Does the Commission have specific plans to increase the use of recycled paper, and to make greater demands on suppliers to use recycled paper? 6. Does the Commission consider it important that recycled paper be white, or would unbleached paper be an environmentally friendly solution?
