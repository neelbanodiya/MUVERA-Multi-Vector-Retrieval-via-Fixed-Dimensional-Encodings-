WRITTEN QUESTION E-0926/06 by Andrzej Szejna (PSE) to the Commission (7 March 2006) Subject: The Constitutional Treaty After the rejection of the Constitutional Treaty in the Netherlands and France the Council announced ‘a period for reflection’. The Commission proposed Plan D for democracy, dialogue and debate, and the Austrian Presidency has the Constitutional Treaty as one of its priorities during the next six months. What kind of detailed actions reaching further than general declarations has the Commission already taken or is the Commission going to take up in 2006 in order to prepare the clear statement required by the Council to undertake political decisions at the beginning of 2007?
