11 May 2000 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION further to Oral Question B5‑218/2000 pursuant to Rule 42(5) of the Rules of Procedure by Cristiana Muscardini, Luís Queiró and José Ribeiro e Castro, on behalf of the UEN Group on the appointment of senior Commission officials European Parliament resolution on the appointment of senior officials The European Parliament, A. having regard to the request made to President Prodi for Parliament to be duly informed about all appointments of senior officials prior to such appointments taking effect, B. having regard to the requests made by the European Parliament according to which the code of conduct must necessarily refer to the guidelines provided by Parliament itself, 1. Condemns the way in which the Commission and its President are proceeding, which demonstrates autonomous decision-making with no account being taken of the views expressed by the Member States; 2. Calls for the principle of fair representation of the different Member States in the various Directorates-General to be safeguarded when the next appointments are made; 3. Calls on the Commission, prior to making any appointments, to provide the European Parliament with full information about the distribution of appointments to A1 and A2 posts among the Member States; 4. Calls on the Commission, in connection with the next budget exercise, to present its own requirements, and hence its own requests, in a detailed document which will set out the activities, programmes and actions that it intends to undertake; 5. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Commission and the Council.
