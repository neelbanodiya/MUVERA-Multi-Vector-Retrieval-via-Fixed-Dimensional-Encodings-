WRITTEN QUESTION E-3021/05 by Sharon Bowles (ALDE) to the Commission (8 August 2005) Subject: Trade impact of the euro Will the Commission state the proportion of GDP of each Member State traded with euro and non-euro EU Member States in each year for the last five years? Will it give the figures using trade in goods, trade in services and trade in goods and services? Will it further as a memo item give the figure for investment income broken down in the same way?
