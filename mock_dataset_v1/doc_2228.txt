Answer given by Mr Frattini on behalf of the Commission (20 February 2008) The Commission is aware that the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) sent a report to Europol on this matter. The investigation of concrete cases of match fixing and other operational activities remain under the responsibility of the Member States. As announced in the White Paper on sport COM(2007)391 final. , the Commission intends to support public-private partnerships representing sports interests and anti-corruption authorities with a view to develop an effective preventative strategy against corruption in sports.
