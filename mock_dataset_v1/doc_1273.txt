On foreign and defence policy, the House welcomes the comprehensive agreement reached with NATO on all outstanding permanent arrangements between the EU and NATO. Parliament also welcomes the decision by the Danish Government not to extradite the leading Chechen politician Achmed Sakajes, as there were no substantial charges retained against him and reiterates its demand that the Russian Federation put all its efforts into finding a political solution to the Chechen crisis. Finally, the House calls for a comprehensive reform of the Union in terms of democratisation and efficiency and draws attention once again to the importance of a successful conclusion of the work of the European Convention.
