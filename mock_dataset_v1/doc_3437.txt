The content of Form D shall be translated by a person qualified to make translations in one of the Member States. 3. The party seeking the enforcement of a judgment given in the European Small Claims Procedure in another Member State shall not be required to have: ( a) an authorised representative; or b) a postal address in the Member State of enforcement, other than with agents having competence for the enforcement procedure. 4. No security, bond or deposit, however described, shall be required of a party who in one Member State applies for enforcement of a judgment given in the European Small Claims Procedure in another Member State on the ground that he is a foreign national or that he is not domiciled or resident in the Member State of enforcement. Article 22 Refusal of enforcement 1.
