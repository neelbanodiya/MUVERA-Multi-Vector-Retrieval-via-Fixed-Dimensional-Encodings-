Question for written answer E-9567/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Pat the Cope Gallagher (ALDE) (16 November 2010) Subject: Restrictions on the maximum permissible level of vitamins and minerals Can the Commission make a statement on its current position relating to a restriction on the maximum permissible levels of vitamins and minerals contained in food supplements? Can the Commission provide information on the scientific methods being used to calculate the maximum doses of vitamins and minerals?
