REGI EGTC Ready to Use - Interinstitutional conference, March 29 The Regulation on the European Grouping of Territorial Cooperation is under revision and the Groupings will be strategic tools for the next programming period 2014-2020. The European Parliament and the Committee of the Regions want to involve the decision-makers of the EU in a dialogue about the EGTC. The conference will be held on 29 March 2012 from 9:30 am to 11:30 am at the Committee of the Regions building in Brussels, Rue Belliard 99-101, Room JDE 52. The session will be open to public.
