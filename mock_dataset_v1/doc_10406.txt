ORAL QUESTION H-0200/04 for Question Time at the part-session in April 2004 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Göran Färm to the Commission Subject: Funding of Info Points and Carrefour information networks What progress has the Commission made with finding a model for the funding of the Info Point Europe and Carrefour information networks ? Various options for funding this information activity have been discussed. Which will the Commission recommend? In this connection, I would stress the importance of creating a sustainable model which guarantees the continued existence of the information networks.
