Nevertheless, according to Article 24(1) of Directive 2006/21/EC, the permit of this installation will have to be reviewed by the competent authorities by 1 May 2012 at the latest in order to include in the permit all requirements of the directive. In addition, the obligations of Article 13(6) of Directive 2006/21/EC for what concerns the limit emission values of weak acid dissociable cyanide are immediately applicable. Member States competent authorities are responsible for the inspection of these installations and so far no information has been received by the Commission on these cyanide concentrations.
