Question for written answer E-005035/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Niki Tzavela (EFD) (23 May 2011) Subject: Freedom of speech Bahrain announced today that it had expelled Frederik Richter, a Reuters news agency correspondent, because of bias in his coverage of the recent repression of demonstrations staged by citizens demanding democratization. Will the Commission say: Is it aware of this incident? How can it ensure freedom of speech in regions suffering from censorship?
