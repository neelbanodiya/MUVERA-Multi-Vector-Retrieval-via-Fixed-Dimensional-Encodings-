Answer given by Ms Ferrero-Waldner on behalf of the Commission (16 June 2009) The Commission, in its contacts with the Government of Pakistan, has stressed the point that fostering stability in Afghanistan is in the interest of all countries in the region. The Commission has stressed the importance of cooperation between Afghanistan and Pakistan. The Commission has further passed the message that the improvement of bilateral relations between Afghanistan and Pakistan is essential and the Commission stands ready to actively contribute to this process. As an example, the Commission hosted a meeting of experts from across the region to identify tangible and viable areas of cooperation in preparation of the Regional Economic Conference of Afghanistan (RECCA) which took place in Islamabad on 13 and 14 May 2009.
