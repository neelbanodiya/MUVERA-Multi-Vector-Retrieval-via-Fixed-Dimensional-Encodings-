WRITTEN QUESTION P-2134/07 by Claude Moraes (PSE) to the Commission (17 April 2007) Subject: Social protection and social welfare support for asylum-seekers and migrants Is the Commission studying or analysing the situation in relation to the level of social protection and social welfare support that the EU countries will provide to destitute asylum-seekers and migrants who may or may not have exhausted their rights to remain within the EU?
