Question for written answer E-4990/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Konrad Szymański (ECR) (2 July 2010) Subject: Promoting the development of research on the brain The Seventh Framework Programme of the European Community (2007‑13) provides for the funding, in the health sector, of research into brain diseases, research that is yielding promising results. Efforts are underway to make 2013 the Year of the Brain. Does the Commission intend to increase funding levels for projects involving research on the brain? Will the Eighth Framework Programme include such funding? How does the Commission intend to support the development of this field of medicine?
