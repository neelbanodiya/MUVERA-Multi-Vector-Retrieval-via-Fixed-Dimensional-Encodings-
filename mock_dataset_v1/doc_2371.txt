WRITTEN QUESTION E-1658/04 by Christopher Huhne (ALDE) to the Commission (17 August 2004) Subject: Brominated flame retardant chemicals Will the Commission outline if it has researched, or is currently researching the issue of brominated flame retardant chemicals (used in computer software), particularly in reference to possible health concerns? Will the Commission forward any evidence collected in regard to such research? Does the Commission have any plans to submit new proposals regarding this issue? If so, is this in light of any research conducted?
