The Preliminary draft budget registers an amount in commitment appropriations and in payment appropriations which is increasing, respectively, to € 121 billion and € 112,5 billion. Before summarising the distribution of expenditure among the different areas of the fisheries sector it is necessary to stress that the European fisheries industry is extremely dependent on the European budget: of the total amount of € 1.1 billion provided by the European Union and national and regional authorities, about € 900 million come from EU budget.
