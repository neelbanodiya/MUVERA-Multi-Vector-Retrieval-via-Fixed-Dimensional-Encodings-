2002/C 205 E/201 WRITTEN QUESTION P-0676/02 by Konstantinos Hatzidakis (PPE-DE) to the Commission (1 March 2002) Subject: Cancellation of tendering procedure for the Thessaloniki underground Three years after the Greek Parliament approved under law 2717/99 the contract for the construction of the Thessaloniki underground, the Government is now seeking to cancel the project and to issue invitations to tender for the construction of a tramline. Is the Commission aware of the Government's intentions? What has caused the delays to date in the construction of the Thessaloniki underground?
