WRITTEN QUESTION E-6892/08 by Jim Allister (NI) to the Commission (22 December 2008) Subject: Pork stocks Does the Commission confirm or deny reports that the Irish Government has offered to buy millions of euros worth of wasted pork stocks from processing plants, in light of the recent dioxin scare, and can it comment upon whether such a move would be in keeping with EU rules on state aid and competitiveness?
