WRITTEN QUESTION E-1029/08 by Manolis Mavrommatis (PPE-DE) to the Commission (4 March 2008) Subject: Information for the public regarding the use of antibiotics In view of a recent survey concerning the frequency with which antibiotics are being taken in the EU Member States, how is the Commission assisting Member States in implementing their public information policies regarding the dangers of the excessive and protracted use of antibiotics? Have budget appropriations been earmarked for information campaigns in this area?
