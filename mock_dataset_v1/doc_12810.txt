351 audits were carried out by 31 December 2009 over 520 participations, for a total EU grant amount of EUR 103 183 897. This means that by 31 December 2009, the Directorate General for Enterprise and Industry has audited 27 % of its beneficiaries, and 31.4 % of total EU grant commitment in financial terms. Out of the 520 participants audited, 327 presented ineligible costs in their statement of expenses. However for 204 out of these 327 cases the error rate was below 2 %. It is also noteworthy that of the 520 participants audited, 155 participating beneficiaries had declared a lower amount of costs than were admitted by the eligibility rules. The Directorate General for Enterprise and Industry has a team of 8 persons, corresponding to 7.70 full time equivalents, to ensure all activities related to external ex-post audits, including the performance of ex-post audits on the spot and the management of and quality assurance for ex-post audits that are performed by external audit service providers. An amount of EUR 937 315 was committed in 2009 for the provision of external audit services.
