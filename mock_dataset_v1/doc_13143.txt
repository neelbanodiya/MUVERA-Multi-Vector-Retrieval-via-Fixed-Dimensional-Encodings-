Amendment 5 ARTICLE 1, SOLE PARAGRAPH 3 Article 10, Paragraph 3 (Directive 91/440/EWG) (b) Paragraph 3 is deleted . Paragraph 3 is replaced by the following : "3. Independently of operating mode, railway undertakings within the scope of Article 2 shall, for the purpose of operating international transport goods services, be granted, on equitable conditions, the access sought by them to the Trans-European Rail Freight Network within the meaning of Article 10a and Annex I, and, in any event not later than 1 January 2006, access to the whole network." Justification Since, as explained in Amendment 1 and its justification, the special 'Trans-European Rail Freight Network' is to be established, the Commission proposal to delete the reference to the special network should itself be deleted. At the same time, however, a firm deadline for the transition from the special network to the opening up of the whole network for international freight transport must be fixed, and must correspond to the deadline proposed by the rapporteur for implementing the directive in accordance with Amendment 11.
