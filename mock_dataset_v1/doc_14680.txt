Question for written answer E-010711/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Nikolaos Chountis (GUE/NGL) (21 November 2011) Subject: Contribution by eurozone Member States to the PSI Paragraph 12 of the Euro Summit statement of 26 October 2011 notes that: ‘Eurozone Member States would contribute to the PSI package up to 30 bn euro’. In view of the above, will the Commission say: Can it clarify how this measure will be implemented? What kind of ‘return’ will Member States which contribute to the PSI package receive?
