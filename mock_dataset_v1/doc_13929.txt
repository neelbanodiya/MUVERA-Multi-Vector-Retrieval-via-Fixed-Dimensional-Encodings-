WRITTEN QUESTION E-6623/09 by Georgios Papanikolaou (PPE) to the Commission (13 January 2010) Subject: Internet grooming The last few years have seen an increase in the phenomenon of Internet grooming in the EU. This is a recent phenomenon involving the seduction of children which takes advantage of young people's familiarity with computers and the new technologies. It is becoming extremely widespread in a number of Member States, including Greece. In view of the above, will the Commission say: 1. What initiatives has it taken to curb Internet grooming? 2. Does it have specific data on the extent of the phenomenon in the EU as a whole and in Greece in particular?
