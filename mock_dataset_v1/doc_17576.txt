The points raised below stem from an examination of the implementation of the budget, the discharge conclusions adopted by Parliament, reports on budgetary management by the European Court of Auditors, and Parliament conclusions on the fight against fraud. Earmarking 1. Grants awarded under budget lines 15040103 (subsidy for cultural organisations advancing the idea of Europe) and 15060103 (grants to organisations advancing the idea of Europe) have in the past been earmarked. However from 2006 onwards earmarking is ruled out by the Financial Regulation, and access to these grants is instead decided on the basis of a call for proposals.
