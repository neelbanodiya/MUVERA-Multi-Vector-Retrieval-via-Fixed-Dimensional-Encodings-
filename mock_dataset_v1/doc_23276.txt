Question for written answer E-010644/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Giovanni La Via (PPE) (20 December 2010) Subject: State of implementation of Heading 2 — Preservation and management of natural resources — of the general EU budget for 2010: budget item 05 02 05 In its December 2009 part-session, Parliament adopted the EU general budget for 2010, involving a total of EUR 141.4 billion in expenditure commitments and EUR 122.9 billion in payments. Under Heading 2 — Preservation and management of natural resources — EUR 59 498 833 302 were allocated for expenditure commitments and EUR 58 135 640 809 for payments. Under item 05 02 05 — Sugar — EUR 1 500 000 were allocated for expenditure commitments and EUR 1 500 000 for payments. Given that the agricultural year came to an end on 15 October 2010, can the Commission say: 1. what the level of implementation of this budget item has been; 2. how and for what activities the above amounts have been used; 3. what amounts were allocated and how much was actually implemented in each Member State; 4. whether any resources have remained unused; 5. if so, what is the amount of those unused resources and why were they not used?
