Answer given by Mr Füle on behalf of the Commission (25 August 2010) The Commission understands from Canada’s Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade that the Honourable Member’s information about Canadian UNRWA The United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East. funding is not correct. Canada’s Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade informs the Commission that so far in 2010, Canada has provided 15 million Canadian dollars for humanitarian work in the West Bank, Gaza and Lebanon through UNRWA. In 2009, Canada contributed a total of 20 million Canadian dollars to help UNRWA provide assistance such as food and shelter to Palestinian refugees in need. Canada’s Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade further informs the Commission that, in accordance with Canadian anti-terrorism legislation, UNRWA ensures that Canadian funds do not benefit Canadian-listed terrorist groups, including Hamas.
