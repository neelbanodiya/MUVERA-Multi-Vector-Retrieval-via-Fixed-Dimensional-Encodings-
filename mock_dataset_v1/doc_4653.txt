WRITTEN QUESTION E-0095/07 by Frank Vanhecke (ITS) to the Council (19 January 2007) Subject: Work programme of the German Presidency of the Council In its work programme on home affairs and justice, the German Presidency of the Council states that it wishes to continue the joint repatriation flights and that Frontex must support these flights (Innenpolitisches Arbeitsprogramm der deutschen EU‑Ratspräsidentschaft, January 2007, p. 8). Can the Council provide an overview of the number of joint repatriation flights which have so far taken place and their total cost?
