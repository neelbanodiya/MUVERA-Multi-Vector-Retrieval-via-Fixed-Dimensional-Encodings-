New sources of funding Lastly, the report says that new funding must be found from sources such as humanitarian funds for responding to climate-related disasters, Common Foreign and Security Policy (CFSP) funds and the Instrument for Stability in the context of preventive security policy or in response to climate-related security threats or conflicts. Green taxes and public-private partnerships would be another possibility.
