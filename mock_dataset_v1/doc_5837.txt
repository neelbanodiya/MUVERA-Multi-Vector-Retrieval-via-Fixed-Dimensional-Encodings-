We should not insist on the wording adopted at first reading ‘the highest possible degree of independence from the Governments of Member States’. However, the wording in the common position ‘Member States shall designate one or more competent bodies with the function of regulatory authorities’ offers sufficient scope to do justice to the intention of this paragraph. The wording adopted here, on the other hand, leads to lack of clarity as to competences and responsibilities.
