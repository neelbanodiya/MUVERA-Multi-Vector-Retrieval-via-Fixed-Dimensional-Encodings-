WRITTEN QUESTION E-3591/08 by Dimitrios Papadimoulis (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (25 June 2008) Subject: Siting of a marina at Aktion According to the Commission's answer to my Written Question E‑412/07 on the siting of a marina at Aktion, Prefacture of Preveza: the Commission stated ‘that it would nevertheless request supplementary information from the Greek authorities, with regard to the proper implementation of the marina project in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the development permit as well as any impacts on the Natura 2000 site linked to the construction work’. Have the Greek authorities forwarded the supplementary information requested by the Commission? What view does the Commission take of this information? What kind of measures does it intend to take to protect the vulnerable environment of the Ambrakian gulf?
