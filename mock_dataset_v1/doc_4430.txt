IMCO Vote on the enforcement of consumer protection The plenary is to endorse the agreement reached with the Council by the IMCO negotiators on a regulation aimed at abolishing the barriers to cross-border cooperation between public enforcement authorities to detect, investigate and bring about the cessation of intra-EU infringements of the laws which protect consumers' interests (Rapporteur: Mitro Repo).
