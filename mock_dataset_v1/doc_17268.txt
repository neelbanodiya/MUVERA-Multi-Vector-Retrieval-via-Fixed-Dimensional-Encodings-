The purpose of the present proposal is to reinforce the controls on intra-EU trade in sheep and goats so as to align these requirements to those approved for animal species susceptible to the same diseases. This proposal is part of a package of legislative activities of the Commission to prevent the spread of major infectious diseases, if they would occur in the EU. This proposal has no financial implications for the budget of the EU. The Commission is obliged to consult the European Parliament on completing the EU list of foodstuffs authorised for irradiation.
