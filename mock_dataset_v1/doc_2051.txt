From 15:30 to 17:00 a public debate will take place in the Parliament's plenary chamber on the theme of the day, with Members of the European Parliament. This year's "traditional May European events" in Strasbourg will highlight topics such as the German presidency, the accession of Bulgaria and Romania and the 50th anniversary of the Rome Treaty. Many other events are being held at the European Parliament as part of this Open Day, including a first "Europa 2007" stamp day, with a special post office.
