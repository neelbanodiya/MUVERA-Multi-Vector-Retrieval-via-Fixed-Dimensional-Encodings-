WRITTEN QUESTION E-0627/06 by Elly de Groen-Kouwenhoven (Verts/ALE) to the Commission (20 February 2006) Subject: Need for improvements in the Bulgarian Underground Resources Act Under the present Bulgarian Underground Resources Act the government is authorised to grant concession contracts for the extraction of underground resources without tender. The legislative programme of the Bulgarian Government for January-June 2006 does not include improvements to the Underground Resources Act. 1. Will the Government of Bulgaria continue to grant concession contracts for the extraction of underground resources without tender? 2. Will Bulgaria change the Underground Resources Act before becoming an EU member and put an end to the admissibility of concession contracts for extraction of underground resources without tender? 3. What steps will the Commission take to urge Bulgaria to improve the Underground Resources Act, making it clear that the granting of concession contracts for extraction of underground resources without tender is inadmissible?
