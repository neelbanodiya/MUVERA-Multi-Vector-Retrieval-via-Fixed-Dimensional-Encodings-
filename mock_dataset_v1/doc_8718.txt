REGI Adoption of the report by Mr Arnaoutakis On 2 December, the REGI Committee adopted unanimously the report on the "Proposal for the revision of article 55 of the Council Regulation (EC) N°1083/2006 laying down general provisions on the European Regional Development Fund, the European Social Fund and the Cohesion Fund and repealing Regulation (EC) N°1260/1999". The article 55 of the regulation refers to ‘revenue-generating project’ and aims at simplifying as much as possible the management of small projects (under 1 mln EUR) co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF) and the Cohesion Fund or operations co-financed by the European Social Fund (ESF), by lowering the administrative burden in the spirit of proportionality. 20081203CAN43558 AVC/2008/0186
