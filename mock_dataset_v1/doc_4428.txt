In order to improve the ability of Member States to evaluate the results of anti-trafficking policies, the Commission presented on the occasion of the Anti-Trafficking Day an Assessment Manual ‘Measuring responses to trafficking in human beings in the European Union: an assessment manual’ is available on the JLS website: http://ec.europa.eu/justice_home/news/events/news_events_en.htm. containing a list of selected indicators, to be used by Member States to collect data and achieve comparable results. The Commission also received a number of projects submitted under the financial program ‘Prevention of and fight against crime’.
