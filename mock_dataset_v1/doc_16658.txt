WRITTEN QUESTION E-3304/10 by Hans-Peter Martin (NI) to the Commission (10 May 2010) Subject: OLAF investigations in connection with the Structural Funds According to the written answer given by Commissioner Johannes Hahn ( E-6148/09 ), OLAF has completed 12 operational cases relating to the Structural Funds in Greece, with a total financial impact of EUR 140 million, of which EUR 131 million has already been recovered. Since when has the remaining sum of almost EUR 9 million been outstanding, and when were the first instalments repaid? Which people, institutions or companies is OLAF investigating in connection with the Structural Funds? Who are the defaulters? Which areas of the Structural Funds are affected? What sanctions are available to OLAF in order to force defaulters to pay?
