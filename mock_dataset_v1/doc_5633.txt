Adopted by EP: 23.3.2006 Adopted by EP: 21.2.2008 Adopted by EP: 20.11.2008 Looking ahead Measures to improve conciliation of family life and work In the next few months, Parliament will be scrutinizing proposed changes in legislation which aim to improve work-life balance. All Member States would have to guarantee a minimum maternity leave of 18 weeks, instead of the current 14 weeks. Self-employed women will also get access to maternity leave.
