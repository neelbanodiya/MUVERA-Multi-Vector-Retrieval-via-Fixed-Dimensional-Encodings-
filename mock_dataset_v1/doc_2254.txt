2004/C 65 E/113 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1881/03 by Christopher Huhne (ELDR) to the Commission (6 June 2003) Subject: Intra-EU foreign direct investment Will the Commission update the figures given in answer to E-0033/02 OJ C 160 E, 4.7.2002, p. 171 . to include the latest available year? It should provide figures on a comparable basis to those given for the European Union for intra-EU foreign direct investment (in graph 12) of the report from the Commission Economic reform: report on the functioning of Community product and capital market COM(2001) 736 final. . Is the Commission also able to provide a series including reinvested earnings? Will the Commission also give the figures for each individual EU Member State that is not yet in the euro?
