ORAL QUESTION H-0206/08 for Question Time at the part-session in April 2008 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Bernd Posselt to the Commission Subject: Child-raising salary and demography Has the Commission looked, as it promised in its answer to a previous question, at the impact of a child-raising salary on population trends in those countries which have such an allowance (e.g. Norway)? Has the Commission been in contact with NGOs working in this field?
