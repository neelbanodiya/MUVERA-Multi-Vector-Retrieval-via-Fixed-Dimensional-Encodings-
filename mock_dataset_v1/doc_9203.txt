Question for written answer E-006410/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Angelika Werthmann (NI) (30 June 2011) Subject: Greece a net contributor? Greece joined the European Community in 1981. 1. Can the Commission give an idea of when (which year) it assumes Greece might move from the status of net beneficiary to that of net contributor to the EU? 2. Does the Commission consider this will be possible in the next 10 years? 3. Is the Commission conducting simulations regularly testing the various scenarios possible as answers to questions 1 and 2?
