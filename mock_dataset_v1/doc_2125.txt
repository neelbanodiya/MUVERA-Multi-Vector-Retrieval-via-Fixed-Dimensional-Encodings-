WRITTEN QUESTION E-1213/09 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (26 February 2009) Subject: Ireland and implementation of cancer screening programmes Could the Commission indicate what response it received from the Irish authorities in reply to its 2007 request to complete a written survey on implementation of the 2003 Recommendation on cancer screening so as to assist the Commission to produce a report on implementation of cancer screening programmes in all Member States? What is the Commission's assessment of the impact of the failure by some Member States to comply with this request?
