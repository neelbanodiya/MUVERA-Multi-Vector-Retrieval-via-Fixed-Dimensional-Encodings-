WRITTEN QUESTION E-1017/08 by Paul van Buitenen (Verts/ALE) to the Commission (29 February 2008) Subject: JRC's use of contract staff and compliance with Staff Regulations provisions concerning the nature of the duties assigned to them 1. Would the Commission state the ratio of contract staff to its total staff and give the same figures for the JRC, indicating the number of contract staff employed at each JRC site as a percentage of total staff? 2. Is the Commission able to confirm that, when the JRC has recourse to contract staff, it takes full account of, and abides by, the provisions of the Staff Regulations specifying the nature of the tasks which may be assigned to staff covered by contracts of this kind? 3. What checking mechanisms apply in this domain? 4. Who is responsible for carrying out the checks in question?
