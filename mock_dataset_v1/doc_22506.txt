The general purpose of this instrument is to support and complement the efforts of the Member States for the protection, primarily of people but also of the environment and property, including the cultural heritage, in the event of natural and man-made disasters, acts of terrorism and technological, radiological or environmental accidents and to facilitate reinforced cooperation between the Member States in the field of civil protection.
