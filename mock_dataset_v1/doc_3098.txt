ORAL QUESTION H-0466/02 for Question Time at the part-session in June 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Nelly Maes to the Council Subject: Peace process in Turkey Now that the PKK has been included on the list of terrorist organisations, the Kurds are very anxious because the Turkish authorities see this as an opportunity to step up their oppressive measures against the Kurds. Can the Council explain whom it regards as its interlocutor in the peace process on the Kurdish side, now that the PKK has been included on the list of terrorist organisations?
