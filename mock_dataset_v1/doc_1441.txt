ORAL QUESTION H-0741/07 for Question Time at the part-session in October 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Hélène Goudin to the Council Subject: Over-fishing in the Baltic Sea In July the Commission decided to halt Polish cod fishing in the Eastern Baltic for the remainder of 2007. Spokesmen for the Polish government have said that they do not intend to punish fishermen who violate this fishing ban. What measures does the Presidency consider that the EU should take against countries which openly state that they do not propose to ensure the enforcement of fishing quotas joint agreements in this area?
