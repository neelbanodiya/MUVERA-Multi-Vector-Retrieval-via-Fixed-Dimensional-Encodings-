Those procedures can only be resumed following the examination of compliance by the EC with the panel report, through an arbitration procedure. The procedures allowing the imposition of sanctions would take approximately nine months. The US and the EU continued technical discussions in 2008, with a last round of discussions in October 2008. The US is reportedly currently considering the way forward. The Commission hopes that the US decides to come back to the technical discussions rather than opting for further litigation.
