The Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism had appointed Georg Jarzembowski rapporteur at its meeting of 21 February 2002. The committee considered the Commission proposal and draft report at its meetings of 11 July 2002, 4 November and 27 November 2002. At the latter meeting it adopted the draft legislative resolution by 34 votes to 12, with 3 abstentions.
