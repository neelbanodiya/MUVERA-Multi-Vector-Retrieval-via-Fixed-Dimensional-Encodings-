Looking ahead Telecom package Proposed reforms of existing legislation on electronic communications (such as mobile and fixed telephony, broadcasting, internet, etc.) are also due to return to the EP. The telecom package aims to improve competition, to eliminate barriers that remain in the already liberalised market and to promote investment in next-generation networks, while guaranteeing consumer protection. The reform also covers the management of radio spectrum.
