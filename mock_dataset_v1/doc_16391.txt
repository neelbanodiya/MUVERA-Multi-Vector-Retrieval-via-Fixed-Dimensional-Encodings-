9999/C Q999 E/035 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1249/04 by Christopher Huhne (ELDR) to the Commission (15 April 2004) Subject: Foreign direct investment by sector Will the Commission break down foreign direct investment by sector showing the total invested in each Member State in each year for the last ten years by manufacturing and by non-manufacturing sectors? Will it provide a series showing the share of each Member State for both manufacturing and non-manufacturing over the ten years?
