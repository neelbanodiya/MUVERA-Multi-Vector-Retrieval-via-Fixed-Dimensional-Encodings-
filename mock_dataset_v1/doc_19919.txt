WRITTEN QUESTION E-2234/06 by Sharon Bowles (ALDE) to the Commission (19 May 2006) Subject: Competition law assistance In respect of the Commission notice on immunity from fines and reduction of fines in cartel cases (2002) OJ C 45/3, 19 February 2002 (hereafter the 2002 leniency notice): 1. Since 14 February 2002, how many cartel investigations has the Commission initiated? How many of those investigations were initiated following the receipt of a leniency application? 2. What is the nationality of the corporate headquarters of the undertakings who have made leniency applications under the 2002 leniency notice since 14 February 2002? 3. From which industrial sectors have leniency applications been made under the 2002 leniency notice since 14 February 2002? 4. In how many cases has the Commission received a leniency application made under the leniency notice and transferred the case to a national competition authority or a number of national competition authorities? 5. In how many cases has a leniency applicant, having made a national leniency application to a national competition authority or national competition authorities, had the leniency application transferred to the Commission? 6. In how many cases has DG Competition received a leniency application under the 2002 leniency notice where, to the Commission's knowledge, the applicant has also made a leniency application to the competition authorities of the United States? 7. Since 14 February 2002 have any conditional offers of immunity been withdrawn, and if so in what circumstances?
