WRITTEN QUESTION E-4277/09 by Gerben-Jan Gerbrandy (ALDE) , Dirk Sterckx (ALDE) and Sophia in 't Veld (ALDE) to the Commission (7 September 2009) Subject: Compensation for the impact of dredging the Western Scheldt in the light of EU nature legislation 1. In April 2009, the Netherlands Government proposed measures to compensate for the impact on nature of dredging the Western Scheldt. Do these comply with European nature legislation? 2. Is it possible to begin dredging the Western Scheldt before the compensation measures are taken, or should both operations take place simultaneously?
