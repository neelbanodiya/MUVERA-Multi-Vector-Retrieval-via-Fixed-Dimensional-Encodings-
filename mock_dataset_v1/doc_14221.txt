In general, how such trust marks and seals of approval are awarded is a matter for national regulation. As such it is a matter for national authorities and courts to regulate such awards and the awarding bodies. Ultimately, the interpretation and enforcement of the directive is a matter for the national authorities and courts in the Member States — the Commission has no role in interpretation or enforcement. The directive had to be fully transposed into domestic law by 12 December 2007. As of mid‑February legislation transposing the directive has been promulgated in all but two Member States (Spain and Luxembourg).
