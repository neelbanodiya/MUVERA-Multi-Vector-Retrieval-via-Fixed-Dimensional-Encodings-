Answer given by Mr Hahn on behalf of the Commission (6 October 2010) Following the Honourable Member's question, the Commission has made contact with the authorities of Ireland, the United Kingdom and Spain. The Commission can confirm that no expenditure declarations have been received with respect to the company in question for funding from the European Regional Development Fund within the last five years. With regard to the European Social Fund (ESF), B3 Cable Solutions in Ireland has received EUR 1 800 under the Employment and Human Resources Development Operational Programme 2000‑06 — In-Company Training Measure in relation to training provided to employees of the company.
