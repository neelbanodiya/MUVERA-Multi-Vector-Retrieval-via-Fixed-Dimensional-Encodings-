ORAL QUESTION H-1122/05 for Question Time at the part-session in January 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Eoin Ryan to the Commission Subject: Pension rights Can the European Commission make a statement as to the progress being made to enact a new directive dealing with the portability of pension rights in Europe so as to help EU workers acquire, preserve or transfer pension entitlements in the event of professional mobility?
