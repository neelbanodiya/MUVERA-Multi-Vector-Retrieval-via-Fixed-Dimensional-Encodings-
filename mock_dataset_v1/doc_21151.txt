These NGOs have followed closely the cases of individuals who have recently been arrested for homosexuality in Morocco. Following these arrests, the AMDH launched a campaign for the decriminalisation of homosexuality. The Commission’s relations with Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries are on a regional rather than a bilateral basis. There is a regular EU‑GCC dialogue at senior official and ministerial level in which the EU raises with the GCC countries their human rights obligations under international law. The Kuwaiti law criminalising transgender people mentioned by the Honourable Member has been a subject of concern among EU Heads of Mission in the country. Accordingly, the EU intends to remind Kuwait of its obligations at the next opportunity.
