This item is related to the Court of Auditor's Report No 3/2004 Special Report No 3/2004 on recovery of irregular Payments under the common agricultural policy (CAP) together with the Commission's replies, OJ C 269, 4.11.2004. concerning a fraud related to concentrated must. OLAF/UCLAF received in 1991 a Communication of irregularity pursuant to Council Regulation (EEC) No 595/91 Council Regulation (EEC) No 595/91 of 4 March 1991 concerning irregularities and the recovery of sums wrongly paid in connection with the financing of the Common agricultural policy and the organisation of an information system in this field and repealing Regulation (EEC) No 283/72, OJ L 67, 14.3.1991. ; the last update No 4 was received on 9 February 2005. According to this last version, the irregularity started in 1990 and took end the same year. The amount of monies defrauded is estimated at EUR 8253022 million, still to be wholly recovered. The three involved operators have successfully disputed the Recovery Orders sent to them by the Greek Paying Agency (Appeal Court of Tripolis, 2003). The file is still under consideration in the First Instance Tribunal of Athens. In the meantime, the three companies have gone bankrupt. This file has been processed by OLAF and the Commission's DG responsible for Agriculture (DG AGRI) in the Framework of the Task Force Recovery for pre 1999 cases. A last bilateral meeting between Greece and the Commission took place on 5 September 2006. The Greek authorities undertook to forward the last available information within one month. In the light of this information, a Final Commission Decision on this case will be adopted at a later stage. 8.
