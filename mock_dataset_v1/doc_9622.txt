Answer given by Mr Piebalgs on behalf of the Commission (2 October 2008) The Commission in its answer to the Honourable Member's Written Question P‑1650/06 Available at http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB indicated that it was studying the proper implementation of Directive 2001/77/EC on the promotion of electricity from renewable energy sources Directive 2001/77/EC of the Parliament and of the Council of 27 September 2001 on the promotion of electricity produced from renewable energy sources in the internal electricity market, OJ L 283, 27.10.2001. in the Region of Castilla León based on three complaints regarding the alleged incorrect transposition or application of Directive 2001/77/EC Complaints 2006/4196, 2006/4197, 2006/4198. . Subsequent to providing the answer to the Honourable Member on 12 May 2006, the complainants informed the Commission of substantial positive development experienced regarding the subject of their complaint. Consequently the complainants have withdrawn their complaints and the Commission closed these three cases on 12 October 2006.
