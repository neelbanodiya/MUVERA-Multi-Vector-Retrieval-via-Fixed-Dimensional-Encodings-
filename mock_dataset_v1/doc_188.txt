WRITTEN QUESTION P-5181/08 by Maria Eleni Koppa (PSE) to the Commission (23 September 2008) Subject: Olympic Airways It appears from reports in the press that the Commission is to approve the Greek Government's plan to close down Olympic Airways. — Is the proposed plan consistent with Community law? — Were talks held with labour and management beforehand? — How secure are workers' jobs in the new company which will be formed? — Will the new company provide services to remote areas of the mainland and the islands? Can the Commission provide details of the plan in regard to the above?
