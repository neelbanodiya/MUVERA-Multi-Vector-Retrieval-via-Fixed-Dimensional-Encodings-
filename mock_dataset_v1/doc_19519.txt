9999/C Q999 E/036 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1250/04 by Christopher Huhne (ELDR) to the Commission (15 April 2004) Subject: Payments record Will the Commission state, for each year for the last five years, what proportion of money that the Commission owes to suppliers for goods and services has been paid within the time limits set out in EU legislation designed to discourage late payments? What measures is the Commission taking to improve the situation? What other targets for timely payment has the Commission set concerning the EU budget, and how successful has it been in meeting them?
