To improve the budgetary control of the Police College, MEPs have drawn up an action plan, to be introduced by the College's director before the end of June. The Budgetary Control Committee will now monitor further improvements in the College. Parliament will decide whether to grant discharge in October, depending on the results. All other EU agencies were granted discharge. French MEP Véronique Mathieu (EPP) was the rapporteur on the agencies' budgets.
