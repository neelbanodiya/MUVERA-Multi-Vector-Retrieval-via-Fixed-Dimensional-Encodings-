DEVE ACP-EU parliament 24-28 November ( Port Moresby ): The ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly meets in Papua New Guinea, a chance for European and ACP MPs to dialogue on key development issues and make political recommendations with a view to achieving the objectives of the Cotonou Partnership Agreement. On the agenda this time: aid effectiveness, Economic Partnership Agreements, child labour, Millennium Development Goals, the food crisis and lots more.
