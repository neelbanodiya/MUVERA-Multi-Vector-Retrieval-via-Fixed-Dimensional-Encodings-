having regard to its resolution on the situation in Turkmenistan of 15 March 2001, - having regard to its resolution on Kazakhstan and Central Asia on 13 February 2003, - having regard to the EC Strategy Paper for Central Asia 2002-2006, - having regard to the EU Statements on Turkmenistan of 19 December 2002, 20 March 2003 and 3 April 2003, - having regard to the EU Presidency Declarations on Turkmenistan of 10 December 2002 and 20 January 2003, - having regard to the resolution of the United Nations Commission on Human Rights on the situation of human rights in Turkmenistan of 16 April 2003, - having regard to the fact that Turkmenistan has obligations under a number of International Conventions and Treaties on human rights including the Convention against Torture and Other Cruel, Inhuman and Degrading Treatment of Punishment, On Turkmenistan A. whereas a climate of repression and extreme intolerance of dissent in Turkmenistan is leading to unacceptable restriction of political and civil liberties, recurring imprisonment of conscientious objectors, persecution of religious minorities, tight control of the media and curtailment of freedom of expression, B. whereas there are reports of scores of men, women and children being detained, harassed and evicted from their homes, and reports of torture and ill-treatment (including malnutrition, lack of medical attention and possibly death) in detention, C. whereas unfair trials have taken place without outside observers, and family members of persons held in custody have been threatened and had property seized from them, D. whereas the principle of religious freedom is not respected since all religious activity other than Sunni Islam and the Russian Orthodox Church is restricted by law and minority religious groups face a lot of difficulties, E. whereas the deterioration of the health and education systems in Turkmenistan has led to the closure of important scientific and cultural institutions and the departure of skilled and qualified people, F. whereas a clause to the Constitution of Turkmenistan introduced in August 2003
