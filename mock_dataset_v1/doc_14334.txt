Answer given by Mr Piebalgs on behalf of the Commission (19 January 2007) Following its commitments during the accession negotiations Bulgaria closed down units 1 and 2 of the Kozloduy Nuclear Power Plant (KNPP) in 2002 and closed units 3 and 4 at the end of 2006. Bulgaria plans to continue operating units 5 and 6 of KNPP. Currently, the Commission has no evidence that Bulgaria will not implement its obligations regarding KNPP as laid down by the Treaty of Accession.
