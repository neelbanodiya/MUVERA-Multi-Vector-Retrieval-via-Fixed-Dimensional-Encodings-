Readmission and the reintegration into the family of the non‑accompanied minors are covered in Spanish law: According to the ‘Real Decreto 2393/2004, del 30 Diciembre, Titulo VIII Articulo 92. Menores extranjeros no acompañados, §4.: The general administration of the state, in accordance with the principle of “regroupation” of minors (under aged) after having listened to the minor, and before having informed the services for protection of the minors, will decide on the repatriation of the minor to his country of origin, or to where his/her parents are or over the staying in Spain. In accordance with the principle of the interest of the minors, a repatriation to the country of origin will only be implemented if the conditions for an effective family “regroupation” of the minor will be given, or an adequate attention from the services of protection for the minors in the country of origin is given. Furthermore, the “Protocole Général entre le Département de l'immigration de la Communauté de Madrid et l'Entraide Nationale du Ministère de Développement Social de la Famille et de la Solidarité du Royaume du Maroc” signed a “Protocolo General” on 3 April 2006 in Madrid, defining the exact actions and competences involved in the Project. This “Protocolo General” is in conformity with the “Memorandum de Entendimiento entre el Reino de Marruecos y el Reino de España sobre repatriación de menores no acompañados” signed on 23 December 2003.
