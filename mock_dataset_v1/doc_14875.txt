Answer given by Ms Kroes on behalf of the Commission (11 July 2008) The Irish authorities have informed the Commission that Structural Funds support has been provided to Waterford Crystal in the course of the 1994‑99 and 2000‑06 programming periods as follows: European Regional Development Fund (ERDF) 1994‑99: EUR 3462326 .87 ERDF 2000‑06: EUR 836671 .81 European Social Fund (ESF) 2000‑06: EUR 82732 .90 However, the Commission did not have contacts with the Irish authorities about the possibility of them granting other kinds of aid to Waterford Crystal.
