Press enquiries: Armelle Douaud (Strasbourg) tel.(33) 3 881 74779 (Brussels) tel.(32-2) 28 43806 e-mail : deve-press@europarl.eu.int Human Rights India - Mumbai bomb attack Joint motion for a resolution on the bomb attacks in Mumbai Doc.: B5-0371/2003 , B5-0376/2003 , B5-0379/2003 , B5-0381/2003 , B5-0386/2003 , B5-0387/2003 Debate: 04.09.2003 Vote: 04.09.2003 Vote In the wake of the terrorist bomb attacks near the Gateway of India and in the Zaveri Bazaar on 25 August which killed and injured many civilians, Parliament adopted a joint resolution strongly condemning the terrorist bomb attacks. The House deplores the deliberate targeting of civilians by terrorists and the resulting loss of innocent lives and injuries. Furthermore, MEPs stress that under no circumstances can any terrorist attack be accepted by the international community. The House, through this resolution, sends its condolences to the relatives of all the victims and expresses its solidarity with the Parliament of India. The House calls on the Indian authorities and, in particular, the Mumbai City authorities, to take all necessary steps to prevent clashes between religious communities. The House also welcomes the fact that the Foreign Minister of Pakistan condemned the bombings. Finally MEPs call for the efforts at rapprochement between India and Pakistan to continue.
