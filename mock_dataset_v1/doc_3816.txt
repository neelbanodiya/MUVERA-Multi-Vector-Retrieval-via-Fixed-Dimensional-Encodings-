ORAL QUESTION H-0707/02 for Question Time at the part-session in October 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Erik Meijer to the Commission Subject: Preparing, buying and using a special bookkeeping system by the European Commission With regard to the matter referred to in written questions E-2557/02 , E-2558/02 and E-2559/02 : Can the Commission confirm that around 1995 the Commission had planned to use 4000 licences from the German software enterprise SAP, specially developed for its purposes? Can the Commission confirm that it finally bought 700 licences from SAP and that only 300 or 400 of these licences were really used? What is the number of licences currently in use? How are those licences actually used?
