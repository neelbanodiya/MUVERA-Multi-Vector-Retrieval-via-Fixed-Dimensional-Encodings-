WRITTEN QUESTION E-4667/05 by Sharon Bowles (ALDE) to the Commission (12 December 2005) Subject: Trials for fraud Will the Commission provide a table which indicates, in respect of each Member State, whether the adjudicating authority on factual matters for those accused of serious fraud are tried, initially, by a court where the proceedings are heard by a magistrate, by a judge sitting alone or by a judge sitting with a jury?
