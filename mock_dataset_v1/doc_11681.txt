WRITTEN QUESTION P-2811/05 by Pedro Guerreiro (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (20 July 2005) Subject: EU-China agreement on textiles and garments of 10 June 2005 — ‘memorandum of understanding’ The ‘memorandum of understanding’ between the Commission and China was signed on 10 June 2005. The reports on the monitoring of imports show that on 11 July 2005 the limits laid down in that memorandum for the whole of 2005 and for all categories had already been exceeded. 1. How does the Commission explain this situation? 2. What urgent measures will it take? 3. What is the Commission's position on this matter?
