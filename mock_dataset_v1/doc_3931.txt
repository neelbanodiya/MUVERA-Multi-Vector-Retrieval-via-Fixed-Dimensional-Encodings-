The Commission is analysing further these impacts, and in this context held a meeting with the concerned Third Countries on 28 September 2011. An instrument on collective redress does not currently exist at EU level. At present, only fourteen Member States have some form of collective redress. Whether consumer organisations in these Member States have a right to bring collective action in order to safeguard the quality of products in which unintended presence of GMO was found is a matter of Member State competence.
