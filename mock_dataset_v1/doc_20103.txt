3 December 2003 FINAL A5-0450/2003 REPORT on the request for defence of parliamentary immunity and privileges made by Olivier Dupuis ( 2003/2059(IMM) ) Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market Rapporteur: Sir Neil MacCormick 332.603 CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE PROPOSAL FOR A EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT DECISION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT ANNEX PROCEDURAL PAGE At the sitting of 26 March 2003 the President of Parliament announced that he had received from Mr Olivier Dupuis a request for defence of his parliamentary immunity in connection with legal proceedings pending before the Rome District Court. Pursuant to Rule 6(3) of the Rules of Procedure, the request was referred to the committee responsible, the Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market. At its meeting of 23 April 2003 the committee appointed Sir Neil MacCormick rapporteur. At its meeting of 17 June 2003 it held an exchange of views on the grounds for and against the defence of immunity and at its meeting of 10 September 2003 it heard Olivier Dupuis, pursuant to Rule 6a(3) of the Rules of Procedure. It considered the draft report at its meetings of 26 November and 2 December 2003 and adopted the proposal for a decision unanimously. The following were present for the vote: Willi Rothley,vice-chairman acting chairman, Sir Neil MacCormick, rapporteur; Uma Maija Aaltonen, Carmen Cerdeira Morterero (for Carlos Candal pursuant to rule 153(2)), Evelyne Gebhardt, Fiorella Ghilardotti, José María Gil-Robles Gil-Delgado, Malcolm Harbour, Lord Inglewood, Angelika Niebler (for Bert Doorn), Elena Ornella Paciotti (for Maria Berger), Ian Twinn (for Marianne L.P. Thyssen) and Diana Wallis. The report was tabled on 3 December 2003.
