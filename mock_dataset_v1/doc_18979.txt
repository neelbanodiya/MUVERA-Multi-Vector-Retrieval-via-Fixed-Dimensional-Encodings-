Welcomes the conclusion of the European Commission that Bulgaria continues to fulfil the Copenhagen political criteria, that its economic structures and performance have again significantly improved and that the level of alignment and implementation of commitments all place Bulgaria on track for accession within the envisaged timeframe; 2. Strongly supports the ongoing reform of Bulgaria's public administration and the amendments to the Civil Service law strengthening the merit principle; 3.
