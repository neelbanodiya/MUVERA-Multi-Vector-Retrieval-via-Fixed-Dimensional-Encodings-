European Development Fund Michiel van HULTEN (PES, NL) Report on the proposal for a Council regulation on a Financial Regulation applicable to the 9th European Development Fund ( COM(2002) 290 – C5-0361/2002 – 2002/0183(CNS)) Doc.: A5-0409/2002 Procedure : Consultation procedure Vote : 17.12.2002 Michiel van HULTEN (PES, NL), for the Budgets Committee, will be tabling a draft resolution on the financial regulation applicable to the 9th European Development Fund. The reporter is calling on the Convention and the prospective IGC tasked with reform of the Treaties to examine the question of incorporating the EDF into the Union budget. Working Time Directives simplified Giuseppe GARGANI (EPP-ED, I) Report on the proposal for a European Parliament and Council directive concerning certain aspects of the organisation of working time (codified version) ( COM(2002) 336 – C5-0297/2002 – 2002/0131(COD)) Doc.: A5-0426/2002 Procedure : Codecision procedure (1 st reading) Vote : 17.12.2002 Giuseppe GARGANI (EPP-ED, I), on behalf of the Legal Affairs Internal Market Committee, recommends the approval of the Commission proposal without amendment on codifying legislation on the organisation of working time. The new directive will supersede the various directive incorporated in it; their content is fully preserved, and they are brought together with only such formal amendments as are required by the codification exercise itself.
