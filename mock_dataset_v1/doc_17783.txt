Calls on the Russian authorities to take seriously the problem of displaced persons from Chechnya, to open the border between Chechnya and the neighbouring republics, to guarantee the refugees essential infrastructures and the supply mechanism necessary for their survival in the respect of the Geneva Convention on Refugees; 9. Urges the Commission, the Council and the Member States to make the necessary humanitarian aid available for the suffering population in Chechnya as well as for the refugees in the neighbouring Republics; in this context calls upon the Russian authorities to grant international humanitarian organisations the access to the territory; 10. Urges the Commission and the Council to support the Republic of Georgia in its efforts to receive and assist refugees from Chechnya; 11. Decides to evaluate the possibility of sending a representative mission of Members to Moscow, Ingushetia and Chechnya to gather objective information and report to Parliament in December; 12. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission, the OSCE, the Duma and Government of the Russian Federation, the President and Government of Georgia, the authorities in Chechnya, and the governments of Ingushetia, Dagestan, North Ossetia and of the member states of the Black Sea Council.
