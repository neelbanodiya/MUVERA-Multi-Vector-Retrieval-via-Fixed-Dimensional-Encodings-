Amendment 23 ARTICLE 1, POINT 1 Article 3, point 5, indent 5 (Directive 91/630/EEC ) - All sows must be given high fibre food as well as high-energy food in order to reduce hunger as well as to provide for the need to chew. The high-energy food can be fed once per day in a single meal but high fibre food must be available for longer periods. - In addition to high-energy food sows must - in line with the principles of nutrition appropriate to need - be given food to reduce hunger as well as to provide for the need to chew. Amendment 24 ARTICLE 1, POINT 1 Article 3, point 5, indent 5a (new) (Directive 91/630/EEC ) - Pregnant sows and gilts must have access to straw or other material capable of imparting a feeling of satiety and satisfying their need for material in which to root.
