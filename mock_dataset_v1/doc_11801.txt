The provision concerning unmarried couples is only applicable in those Member States where couples in this type of relationship have equivalent legal status to married couples. Consequently, this provision does not in any way entail a harmonisation of national laws concerning the recognition of unmarried couples, but simply facilitates the application of the principle of equality of treatment. In order to avert potential abuses, unmarried partners should have to be in a long-term relationship.
