WRITTEN QUESTION P-2650/09 by Hannes Swoboda (PSE) to the Commission (23 April 2009) Subject: Discrimination against European manufacturers of solar energy equipment in Portugal The Portuguese Government has set up a new solar energy support programme with EU funding totalling EUR 100 million. However, the funding has been distributed exclusively by Portuguese banks and has thus far only been used to support projects put forward by Portuguese manufacturers. What steps is the Commission taking to guarantee access to the Portuguese market for all manufacturers?
