WRITTEN QUESTION P-2644/06 by Georgios Karatzaferis (IND/DEM) to the Commission (12 June 2006) Subject: Opening of EU office in occupied Cyprus Is there any possibility that some kind of office (‘technical assistance’, ‘project implementation’ etc.) will be opened after 1 July 2006 in occupied Cyprus to deal with the substantial financial aid which the EU grants to the Turkish Cypriots? Have the Greek and Cypriot governments been informed and have they given their consent to the opening of this office in the occupied sector? Have Commissioners Dimas and Kyprianou been informed and have they also given their consent?
