WRITTEN QUESTION E-5405/08 by Marios Matsakis (ALDE) to the Commission (7 October 2008) Subject: Cyprus banks and EC laws Could the Commission confirm that it has issued the Cyprus Central Bank with an ultimatum relating to non-conformity of Cyprus banks with the EU regulation on wire transfer fees charged to customers? If this is so, will the Commission proceed speedily with taking proper action if the Cyprus banks continue to violate EC laws in the aforementioned way?
