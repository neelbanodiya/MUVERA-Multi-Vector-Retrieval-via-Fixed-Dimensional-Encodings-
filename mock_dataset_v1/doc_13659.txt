Question for written answer E-011020/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Diogo Feio (PPE) (25 November 2011) Subject: Professional mobility On 15 November 2011, Parliament adopted a resolution in which it calls for greater efforts to be made in terms of recognising professional qualifications within the Union, in order to increase the mobility of professionals within the EU. Could the Commission provide the following information: — What specific steps does it intend to take to simplify and speed up the recognition of professional qualifications? — What are the main obstacles encountered and for which professions has it been hardest to gain recognition? What measures has it considered or is it considering taking to overcome these problems? — What advantages for workers and their mobility does it foresee being created by the ‘European Professional Card’, in the event of its adoption?
