The Hemicycle The Hemicycle MEPs are arranged in the Chamber in a semi-circular hemicycle according to political group, ordered from left to right, with smaller groups placed towards the outside. All desks are equipped with microphones, headphones for listening to interpretation and electronic voting equipment. The leaders of the groups sit on the front benches in the centre, and at the very centre is a podium for guest speakers. Translation booths are located behind MEPs and along the sides of the chamber, while public galleries are located in the balconies at the rear. 20081114NOT42031 Seating order in the hemicycle
