DEVE ACP-EU meetings 4-9 April (Prague): Climate change, cultural diversity, the food and financial crises, WTO negotiations, economic partnership agreements, Somalia... These are just a few of the issues on the agenda of the next ACP-EU joint assembly in Prague. The assembly is the parliamentary arm of the EU's longstanding relationship with 78 developing nations from Africa, the Caribbean and the Pacific region. It brings together 156 parliamentarians from the EU and ACP to debate issues of common interest and meets twice a year. 20090401CAN53092 ACP-EU JPA website
