Adopted: 22.4.2009 Independence of auditors MEPs adopted new rules intended to help improve quality audits within the EU and underpin confidence in the functioning of EU capital markets. European auditors and audit firms are required to prove their independence from the management of the audited company. Directive adopted by EP: 28/09/2005 Bank deposits guaranteed up to €100,000 If a European bank fails, citizens' savings will be guaranteed up to €100,000, after Parliament backed legislation raising the guarantee level. The existing minimum guarantee of €20,000 will first rise to €50,000 by 30 June 2009, and then €100,000 by the end of 2010. MEPs also decided that bank customers should be given faster access to their deposits in emergencies and that a mechanism be established for cross-border cooperation in the event of another economic crisis. Simpler, cheaper cross-border payments on the way MEPs adopted a legal framework designed to make cashless payments – such as card transactions, bank transfers and direct debits – simpler and cheaper, paving the way for the creation the single Euro payments area.
