Committee Chairs and Vice-Chairs: the final list Institutions 2007-02-01 - 16:12 The chairs and vice-chairs of the parliamentary committees have been elected at a series of special meetings on Wednesday and Thursday. These elections follow the approval by the plenary session of the membership of each committee. The chairs and vice-chairs are due to serve for second half of the present Parliament's term of office, that is until the next European elections in June 2009.
