The draft regulation also covers GM materials whose authorisations have expired and for which a quantitative method had been validated and published by the European Union Reference Laboratory. To date, no authorisation of genetically modified organisms has been withdrawn in the European Union. In any event, should an EU authorisation be modified, suspended or revoked based, the GM material would be subject to specific measures and would not be subject to the draft regulation.
