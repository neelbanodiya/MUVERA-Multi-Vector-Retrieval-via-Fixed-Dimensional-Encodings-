WRITTEN QUESTION P-5360/08 by Daniel Varela Suanzes-Carpegna (PPE‑DE) to the Council (30 September 2008) Subject: Piracy in Somalia The EU has just approved the establishment of a so-called ‘coordination cell’ to support surveillance and protection operations led by certain Member States along the Somali coast. 1. What specific action has been entrusted to this ‘coordination cell’, and what results are being obtained? 2. What real resources has the EU made available to the Member States in order to achieve this goal? 3. Which Member States are collaborating in this coordination cell, and what resources are they supplying? 4. How does the Council assess the results of this initiative so far?
