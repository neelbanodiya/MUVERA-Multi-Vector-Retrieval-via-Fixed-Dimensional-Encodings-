The evidence gathered during the consultation process has shown that consumers are not confident enough to shop cross-border and that the cost of compliance with differing consumer protection regulations deters businesses from cross-border trade. In order to remedy the situation the Commission aims at tabling a proposal for a Framework Directive on Consumer Contractual Rights in the course of 2008.The Commission encourages the Honourable Member to follow closely further developments in this area.
