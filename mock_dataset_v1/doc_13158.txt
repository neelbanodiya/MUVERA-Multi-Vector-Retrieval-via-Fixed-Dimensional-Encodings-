AGRI McGuinness opinion on Budget 2011 (mandate for the trilogue) The Committee will adopt its opinion for the Budget committee, giving a first assessment of the Draft Budget 2011. The rapporteur welcomes the fact that the margin in Heading 2 is higher than expected, but expresses her concern about the Commission's optimistic assumptions on the evolution of agricultural markets.
