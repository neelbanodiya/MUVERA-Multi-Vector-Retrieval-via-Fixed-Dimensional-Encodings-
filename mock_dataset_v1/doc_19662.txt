reading) The European Parliament, - having regard to the Commission proposal to the European Parliament and the Council ( COM(2001) 35 OJ C 154 E, 29.5.2001, p. 290. ), - having regard to Articles 251(2) and 80(2) of the EC Treaty, pursuant to which the Commission submitted the proposal to Parliament ( C5-0078/2001 ), - having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of the Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism ( A5-0354/2001 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal as amended; 2. Asks to be consulted again should the Commission intend to amend the proposal substantially or replace it with another text; 3. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
