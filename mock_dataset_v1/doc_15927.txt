Answer given by Mr Špidla on behalf of the Commission (2 August 2006) The Commission has already mentioned in different replies to written questions that it intends to table a report on the implementation of the Article 8 of Council Directive 80/987/EEC of 20 October 1980 Council Directive 80/987/EEC of 20 October 1980 on the protection of employees in the event of the insolvency of their employer, OJ L 283, 28.10.1980, as amended by Directive 2002/74/EC of Parliament and of the Council of 23 September 2002, OJ L 270, 8.10.2002. . This report will draw upon the findings of an independent study and Member States' replies to a questionnaire sent by the Commission; it is due to be released once the appropriate stakeholders, and in particular the social partners, have been duly consulted. In the meantime, the High Court of Justice of the United Kingdom (UK) has requested a preliminary ruling from the European Court of Justice (in Case C‑278/05 between — Carol Marylin Robinson and John Burnett against the Secretary of State for Work and Pensions) on the meaning of Article 8 of Council Directive 80/987/EEC and whether the UK Government has properly implemented this article.
