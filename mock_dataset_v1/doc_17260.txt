Regional, Transport & Tourism Statistics on Air Passengers, Freight and Mail Samuli POHJAMO (ELDR, FIN) Report on the Council common position for adopting a European Parliament and Council regulation on statistical returns in respect of the carriage of passengers, freight and mail by air (10011/2/2002 – C5-0451/2002 – 1995/0232(COD)) Doc.: A5-0416/2002 Procedure : Codecision procedure (2nd reading) Debate : 18.12.2002 Over the last few decades air traffic has increased exponentially, raising questions concerning the saturation of airspace, the capacity of airports to meet requirements and the ability of the airline industry to develop procedures and methods enabling it to respect ever tighter environmental controls. In order to ensure the safety of passengers and efficiently manage its enterprises dealing with freight and mail, the European airline industry requires recent and comparable statistics, which will enable them to have a global and detailed view of their activities. Samuli POHJAMO (ELDR, FIN) will be recommending the approval of the Council Common Position without amendment. Progress has been made possible due to the Common Position including an Article on Gibraltar which recognises that the regulation will not apply to the Gibraltar airport ”until the arrangements in the Joint Declaration made by the Foreign Ministers of the Kingdom of Spain and the United Kingdom on 2 December 1987 come into operation”. Women’s Rights Equal Opportunities – Support for more female MEPs Rodi KRATSA-TSAGAROPOULOU (EPP-ED, GR) Report on the Commission report on Equal Opportunities for women and men in the European Union, 6th annual report ( COM(2002) 258 – C5-0336/2002 - 2002/2180(COS)) Doc.: A5-0403/2002 Procedure : Consultation paper Debate : 19.12.2002 Mrs Rodi KRATSA-TSAGAROPOULOU (EPP-ED, GR) for the Women's Rights and Equal Opportunities Committee will be presenting a non-binding draft resolution on The Commission’s Sixth Annual Report on equal opportunities for men and women in 2001. Overall, the Committee welcomes the efforts made by the Commission’s to improve equal opportunities in the Union. The Commission’s Report contains chapters on the strategic framework programme for improving equal opportunities 2001-2005, the legislative evolution on equal opportunities, enlargement, external relations including development policy and the perspective for 2002.
