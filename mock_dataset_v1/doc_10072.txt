ORAL QUESTION H-0233/06 for Question Time at the part-session in April 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Philip Bushill-Matthews to the Council Subject: Working Time Directive Given the difficulties of obtaining agreement on the issue of the individual opt-out from the Working Time Directive 93/104/CE , will the Council please consider trying to secure separate agreement on the specific issue of on-call time? 1 OJ L 307, 13.12.1993, p. 18.
