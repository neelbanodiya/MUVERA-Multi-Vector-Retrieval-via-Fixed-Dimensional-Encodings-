The Parliament's own initiative report on the Commission's evaluation activities A5-0284/2002 of 11 September 2002. refers repeatedly to the annual evaluation review. The same report also ‘congratulates the Commission on its evaluation website which, in a well-arranged way, presents key documents and links to other evaluation websites.’ In response to the Parliament's invitation ‘to continue developing its dissemination of information concerning evaluation activities with regular updates’, the Budget Directorate-General further improved its website http://europa.eu.int/comm/budget/evaluation/index_en.htm by providing a mid-year update of the annual evaluation review, as well as a list of hyperlinks to the most recent evaluation reports that have been published across the Commission. Concerning the publication of the Commission's impact assessment procedures, the Honourable Member is referred to the reply given to his Written Question E‑3570/05 .
