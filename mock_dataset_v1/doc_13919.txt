Answer given by Mr Barnier on behalf of the Commission (30 July 2010) The recent events in sovereign debt markets have prompted increased scrutiny on the market on credit default swaps on sovereign debt (sovereign CDS). As announced in its 2 June 2010 Communication on ‘Regulating financial services for sustainable growth’ COM(2010)301 final. the Commission intends to propose in September 2010, appropriate measures on short-selling, including the issue of sovereign CDS. A public consultation to this end was carried out between 10 June and 10 July 2010.
