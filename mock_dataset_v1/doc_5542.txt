Joint answer given by Mr Cioloş on behalf of the Commission Written questions : E-003241/11 , E-003242/11 (10 May 2011) According to the results of the Farm Structure Survey, in 2007 there were more than 13.7 million managers of farm holdings in the EU‑27 A table is sent directly to the Honourable Member and the Parliament's Secretariat. . The manager of the holding, according to the definition for surveys on the structure of agricultural holdings Commission Decision 2000/115/EC. , is the natural person responsible for the normal daily financial and production routines of running the holding concerned. The available statistics do not allow a distinction between active farmers and others.
