Reply (19 April 2010) In accordance with Article 16(2) of the Treaty on European Union, the Council must consist of a representative of each Member State at ministerial level, who may commit the government of the Member State in question and cast its vote. The Council's Rules of Procedure adopted on 1 December 2009 Council Decision of 1 December 2009 adopting the Council's Rules of Procedure (2009/937/EU), OJ L 325, 11 December 2009, p. 35. , provide that members of the Council may be accompanied by officials who assist them. Their names and functions have to be notified to the General Secretariat in advance (Article 5(3)). It is the reponsibility of each member of the Council to establish the composition of its delegation to Council meetings.
