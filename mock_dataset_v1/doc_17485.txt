The new pilot project "Erasmus style programme for apprentices (line 15 03 01 05) " : it was decided by the European Parliament with an amount of € 2 million allocated as part of the European Parliament' strategic objective of "reinforcement of the Lisbon Strategy". The principal objective of this pilot project is to analyse the main aspects of the apprentice in professional training and the possibility of having a European credit transfer system for professional training, similar to ERASMUS. Five studies are already foreseen On the factors that impede the mobility of apprentices; on launching and developing a European Credit Transfer System; on the methods applicable to this new European Credit Transfer System; on the learning pedagogical organisation and an analysis on the compatibility with the mobility of people in training, a test phase mainly based in an enquiry before national authorities and several actors in the professional training. . 8.
