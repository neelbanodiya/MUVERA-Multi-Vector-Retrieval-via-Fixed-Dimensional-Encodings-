whereas this fighting provoked loss of lives of numerous civilians; whereas the safety of the remaining population is a matter of grave concern, C. whereas the worsening state of security in the capital of Somali, Mogadishu, has prevented national and international NGOs from coping with an unfolding humanitarian catastrophe and responding to emergencies, D. concerned by the prolonged civil war in Somalia and its implications for the peace and reconciliation process in that country, as well as for the security and stability of the Horn of Africa as a whole, E. whereas Ali Mohamed Gedi, the Somali Prime Minister, has resigned after a feud with the President, Abdullah Ahmed Yusuf, which has aggravated the political impasse in the country, F. whereas insurgents of the Union of Islamic Courts exclude all political contact with the government for as long as the Ethiopian army is present in Somalia;
