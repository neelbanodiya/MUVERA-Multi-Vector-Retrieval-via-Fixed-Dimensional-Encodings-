Holders of standard Community model parking cards issued by Member States would benefit from parking facilities available to people with disabilities in any Member State. Furthermore, to facilitate the use of the card, the Commission has set up an informative website http://parkingcard.europa.eu. and published a booklet http://ec.europa.eu/employment_social/docs/en_bookletparkingcard_080522.pdf. informing citizens and national authorities of the mutual recognition of the standardised Community model parking card and the conditions for its use in the various Member States.
