WRITTEN QUESTION E-1862/05 by Alyn Smith (Verts/ALE) to the Commission (24 May 2005) Subject: European Arrest Warrant Does the Commission consider that the category of Computer-Related Crime in Article 2(1) of the Council Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA OJ L 190, 18.7.2002, p. 1 . of 13 June 2002 on the European Arrest Warrant and the surrender procedures between Member States may include offences of speech committed by the placing of information on the Internet and, if so, will the Commission compile a list of all such offences in all the Member States for the information of citizens?
