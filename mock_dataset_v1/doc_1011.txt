Reply (14 February 2012) 1. What was the ‘official meeting’ Mr Van Rompuy attended prior to the Bilderberg conference? That meeting was the EU‑Russia summit in Niznij Novgorod. 2. Was this engagement arranged before or after he accepted the invitation to attend the Bilderberg conference? That engagement was arranged before Mr Van Rompuy accepted the invitation to attend the conference. 3. What expenses did Mr Van Rompuy incur and/or what hospitality did he enjoy whilst at the conference? Mr Van Rompuy received a meal and overnight accommodation. 4. Where did Mr Van Rompuy stay overnight and who paid for his accommodation? See answer to the above question. 5. What ‘discussion session’ did Mr Van Rompuy chair? Mr Van Rompuy chaired a breakfast meeting. 6. Who were the ‘institutional actors’ also present at the event? They were members of some other EU institutions and the national governments of some Member States. 7. What do the minutes of the conference say? Mr Van Rompuy has not received any minutes of the conference. 8. What contributions did Mr Van Rompuy make at the conference? Mr Van Rompuy gave an overview of the current economic situation and the measures being taken by the EU.
