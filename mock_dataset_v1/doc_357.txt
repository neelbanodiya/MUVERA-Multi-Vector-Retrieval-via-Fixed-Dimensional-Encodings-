Question for written answer E-5918/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) (26 July 2010) Subject: Legislation permitting age discrimination The fight against discrimination must include the revision of legislation that permits age discrimination, a phenomenon which particularly affects women. Can the Commission state 1. what studies exist concerning legislation permitting age discrimination; 2. what measures are being taken to change this state of affairs and put an end to such discrimination?
