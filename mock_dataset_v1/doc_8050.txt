CULT The cultural dimensions of the EU's external actions On 2 September, the Committee held a first exchange of views about a future initiative report by Ms. Schaake (ALDE) on "The Cultural dimensions of the EU's external actions". Speakers drew attention to the provisions of the Lisbon Treaty article on culture and the 2005 UNESCO Convention on cultural diversity as well as the context of the establishment of the External Actions Service. A second exchange of views will be held on 26 October. 20100909CAN81868
