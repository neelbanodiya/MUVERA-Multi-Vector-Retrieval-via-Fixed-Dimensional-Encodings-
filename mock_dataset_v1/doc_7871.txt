In addition, Council Regulation (EC) No 3/2008 Council Regulation (EC) No 3/2008 of 17 December 2007 on information provision and promotion measures for agricultural products on the internal market and in third countries, OJ L 3, 5.1.2008. and Commission Regulation (EC) No 501/2008 Commission Regulation (EC) No 501/2008 of 5 June 2008 laying down detailed rules for the application of Council Regulation (EC) No 3/2008 on information provision and promotion measures for agricultural products on the internal market and in third countries, OJ L 147, 6.6.2008. have a set up a regime that allows for the Community part-financing of promotion and information programmes for agricultural products, including milk products, in third countries. These programmes are submitted to the Member States that in turn submit them to the Commission for final approval. The co-financed share of these programmes usually amounts to 50 % of their budget.
