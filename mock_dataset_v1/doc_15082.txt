The budget allocated to this scheme for 2009 amounts EUR 56 million. The number of starting and running programmes at 15 April 2009 is 140. This quantity can be divided as follows: — 88 programmes on the internal market of the European Union, — 52 programmes in third countries outside the EU. Measures concerning export products from outside the European Community, and more specifically from third world countries, don't fall into the scope of the abovementioned Regulation and are addressed in the framework of other EU policies.
