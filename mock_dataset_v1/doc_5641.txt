Looking ahead Protection of authors' rights Parliament will be assessing a proposal to extend the term of protection for sound recordings and performers from the current 50 year term, to 70 years following their death. The extension, according to the proposal, will bring added income to authors, performers and record companies. This would offset losses due to internet piracy and allow companies to spend money on new European performers.
