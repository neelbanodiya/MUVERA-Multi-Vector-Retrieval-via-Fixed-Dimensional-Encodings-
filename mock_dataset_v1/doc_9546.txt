2004/C 78 E/006 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3574/02 by Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (13 December 2002) Subject: Outermost regions and enlargement In the European Parliament's resolution of 25 October 2000 on the outermost regions OJ C 197, 12.7.2001, p. 197 . it is expressly stated that, on the basis of Article 299(2) of the Treaty, all acts which are general in scope should be accompanied by a simplified sheet drawn up by the Interdepartmental Group and outlining their possible impact on the outermost regions and the remedial measures required should that impact prove adverse. Particular attention is also drawn to the financial implications of enlargement. In view of the above, would the Commission provide the following information? — What studies and simplified sheets are currently available concerning the impact which acts general in scope may have on the outermost regions, in particular Madeira and the Azores? — What studies are currently available concerning the possible financial impact of enlargement on the outermost regions (in particular Madeira and the Azores) and what measures are being envisaged in order to counter any detrimental effect?
