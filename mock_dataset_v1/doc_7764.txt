ORAL QUESTION H-0116/02 for Question Time at the part-session in March 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Gary Titley to the Commission Subject: Animal welfare in candidate countries The EU's Zoo Directive (1999/22/EC) is due to be transposed into Member State national law by April 2002. What is the Commission doing to ensure that the directive will be fully and effectively implemented in the Member States? Moreover, in light of the present lamentable animal welfare situation in some applicant countries, such as Romania, how will the Commission ensure that this directive will be fully implemented and respected once the EU enlarges? OJ L 94, 9.4.1999, p. 24.
