WRITTEN QUESTION E-2862/04 by Christopher Heaton-Harris (PPE-DE) to the Commission (12 November 2004) Subject: EU competition disputes It is sometimes said that EU competition authorities place more emphasis on protecting competitors than on protecting consumers, especially in comparison with authorities in other countries, such as they United States. What weight does the Commission believe consumer welfare should be given in resolving EU competition disputes, and what steps would you take to ensure that it is given such weight?
