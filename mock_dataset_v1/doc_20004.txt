WRITTEN QUESTION E-4552/08 by Katerina Batzeli (PSE) to the Commission (11 August 2008) Subject: Implementation of veterinary legislation in Greece The Commission, together with the Greek Government, has drawn up a timetable of measures for the full implementation of veterinary legislation and monitoring procedures. What progress has been made in implementing the necessary measures and in the Commission's view, what are the prospects of them being completed fully and on schedule? What would be the possible consequences of failure to fully implement the timetable?
