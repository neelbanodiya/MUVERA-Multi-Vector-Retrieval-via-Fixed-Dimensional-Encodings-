ITRE Mini-Hearing on "Security of Gas Supply" The Committee is holding a public mini-hearing on the Security of Gas Supply in the framework of the legislative preparatory works on the Commission proposal for a regulation concerning measures to safeguard security of gas supply. The hearing will gather experts in international, regulatory and industrial fields as well as in crisis management. It will take place on 9/11/2009 from 15h00 to 17h00, in room PHS 1A002. 20091027CAN63297 Programme
