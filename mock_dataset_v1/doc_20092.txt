20 November 2001 FINAL A5-0407/2001 REPORT on the communication from the Commission on application of the precautionary principle and multiannual arrangements for setting TACs ( COM(2000) 803 - C5-0106/2001 - 2001/2055(COS) ) Committee on Fisheries Rapporteur: Giorgos Katiforis 286.540 CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT PROCEDURAL PAGE By letter of 1 December 2000 the Commission forwarded to Parliament a communication on application of the precautionary principle and multiannual arrangements for setting TACs ( COM(2000) 803 - 2001/2055(COS)). At the sitting of 15 March 2001 the President of Parliament announced that she had referred the communication to the Committee on Fisheries as the committee responsible ( C5‑0106/2001 ). The Committee on Fisheries appointed Giorgos Katiforis rapporteur at its meeting of 23 January 2001. It considered the Commission communication and the draft report at its meetings of 29 May, 9 October and 20 November 2001. At the last meeting it adopted the motion for a resolution unanimously. The following were present for the vote: Daniel Varela Suanzes-Carpegna chairman; Rosa Miguélez Ramos and Hugues Martin, vice-chairmen; Elspeth Attwooll, Arlindo Cunha, Carmen Fraga Estévez, Ian Stewart Hudghton, Heinz Kindermann, Carlos Lage, Brigitte Langenhagen, Giorgio Lisi (for Antonio Tajani), Albert Jan Maat, John Joseph McCartin, Patricia McKenna, James Nicholson, Camilo Nogueira Román, Struan Stevenson, Catherine Stihler and Margie Sudre. The report was tabled on 20 November 2001. The deadline for tabling amendments will be indicated in the draft agenda for the relevant part-session.
