Question for Question Time H-0433/2010 to the Council Part-session: September II 2010 Rule 116 Liam Aylward (ALDE) Subject: European job creation measures What measures are being adopted at European level and in the Member States to support and encourage efforts aimed at creating jobs? What is being done to tackle unemployment and what measures are in place to provide further access to employment and to the labour market throughout the EU, especially in disadvantaged areas?
