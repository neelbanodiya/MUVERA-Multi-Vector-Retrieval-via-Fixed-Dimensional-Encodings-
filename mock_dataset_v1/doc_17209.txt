WRITTEN QUESTION E-1156/08 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (5 March 2008) Subject: Dental amalgam What is the current situation with regard to the questions on the safety of dental amalgam and alternative dental restoration materials for patients and users that the Commission put to the Scientific Committee on Emerging and Newly Identified Health Risks and the questions on the environmental risks and indirect health effects of mercury in dental amalgam put to the Scientific Committee on Health and Environmental Risks? Which Member States have yet to respond to the Commission with regard to Action 4 of the Mercury Strategy, which covers dental amalgam waste? What action is the Commission now considering in this area?
