Question for written answer E-001907/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Simon Busuttil (PPE) , Georgios Papanikolaou (PPE) and Monika Hohlmeier (PPE) (3 March 2011) Subject: Readmission agreements In the light of the readmission agreements which have been signed by the EU with third countries, is the Commission satisfied that they are being implemented properly and that the third countries which are party to them are respecting their obligations? Does the Commission foresee any efforts to improve the level of implementation of readmission agreements? Moreover, can the Commission provide a timetable for the planned evaluation of readmission agreements and indicate how far it has followed up on the Stockholm programme initiative to establish an implementation monitoring mechanism?
