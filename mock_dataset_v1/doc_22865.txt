WRITTEN QUESTION E-1931/04 by Baroness Sarah Ludford (ALDE) to the Commission (8 September 2004) Subject: Age discrimination - EU Joint Research Centre Can the Commission explain why applicants for post-doctoral posts at the EU Joint Research Centre must be younger than 35 years old? This would appear to contravene the EU prohibition of discrimination on the grounds of age laid down in Directive 2000/78/EC OJ L 303, 2.12.2000, p. 16 . on equal treatment in employment and occupation.
