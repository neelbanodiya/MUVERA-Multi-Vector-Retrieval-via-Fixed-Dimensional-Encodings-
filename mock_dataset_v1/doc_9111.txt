Following the submission of these programmes on 15 September 2011 by the Member States concerned, their ensuing pre-selection by the national authorities and their examination by the Commission, the Commission will adopt a Decision by 15 November 2011 at the latest. This time schedule would reduce by half the normal procedure duration for adopting such a type of co-financed programmes In addition to that, on 19 July 2011 the Commission launched a media campaign in all EU Member States to win back consumer trust in fruit and vegetables.
