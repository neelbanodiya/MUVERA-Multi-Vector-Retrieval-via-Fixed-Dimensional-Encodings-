WRITTEN QUESTION E-2755/05 by Simon Coveney (PPE-DE) to the Commission (22 July 2005) Subject: Fairground and circus trade and the harmonisation of specific social regulations in road traffic Will the Commission maintain the exemption granted to the fairground and circus trade as set out in the Harmonisation of Specific Social Regulations in Road Traffic? If not, will it then be up to each individual Member State to determine the provisions relating to the fairground and circus trade?
