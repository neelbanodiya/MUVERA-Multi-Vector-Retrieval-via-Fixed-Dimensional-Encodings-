WRITTEN QUESTION E-3249/09 by Mary Lou McDonald (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (30 April 2009) Subject: Workers in Waterford Crytsal Can the Commission state what obligations, especially as regards the guaranteeing of pensions, the Irish State has under the Insolvency Directive towards the workers of the Waterford Crystal plant? What is the Commission's view of how the Irish government has met those obligations?
