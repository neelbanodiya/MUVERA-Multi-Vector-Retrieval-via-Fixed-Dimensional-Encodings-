Question for written answer E-3830/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Piotr Borys (PPE) , Artur Zasada (PPE) , Joanna Katarzyna Skrzydlewska (PPE) and Elżbieta Katarzyna Łukacijewska (PPE) (1 June 2010) Subject: EU cultural policy in the Europe 2020 Strategy The Europe 2020 Strategy aims to increase the European Union's competitiveness in relation to other parts of the world. Stimulating creativity in order to increase innovation and entrepreneurialism among EU societies is becoming a key issue. Investing in society is also of fundamental importance, whether by developing cultural infrastructure or by means of initiatives aiming to shape a sense of citizenship. What action will the Commission take in the field of culture as it implements the Europe 2020 Strategy? To what extent does it intend to make use of creative industries in the implementation of the strategy?
