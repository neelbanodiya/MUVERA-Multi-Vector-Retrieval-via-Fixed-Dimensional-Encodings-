A long transitional period, which can go up to the end of 2009, has been foreseen, allowing the maintenance of these substances on national markets until their evaluation by the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) is completed. The Commission and EFSA have prepared guidelines for the preparation of the dossiers in order to facilitate this task for petitioners, who also had the possibility of contacting directly the EFSA secretariat in order to receive further instructions.
