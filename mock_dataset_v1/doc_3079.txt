Answer given by Mr Dalli on behalf of the Commission (14 December 2010) The Commission services are currently finalising the work on preparing the EU list of flavouring substances based on the evaluations by the European Food Safety Authority. Regulation (EC) No 1334 of the European Parliament and of the Council on flavourings and certain food ingredients with flavouring properties for use in and on foods Regulation (EC) No 1334/2008 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 16 December 2008 on flavourings and certain food ingredients with flavouring properties for use in and on foods and amending Council Regulation (EEC) No 1601/91, Regulations (EC) No 2232/96 and (EC) No 110/2008 and Directive 2000/13/EC (Text with EEA relevance), OJ L 354, 31.12.2008. foresees a legal deadline of 31 December 2010. Out of the more than 2000 flavouring substances that have already been evaluated, seven have been concluded to be unsafe.
