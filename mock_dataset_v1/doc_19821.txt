At the sitting of 8 April 2002 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred this draft Council framework decision to the Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs as the committee responsible ( C5‑0152/2002 ). The Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs had appointed Luís Marinho rapporteur at its meeting of 27 February 2002. It considered the draft Council framework decision and the draft report at its meeting of 14 May 2002. At the latter meeting it adopted the draft legislative resolution by 33 votes to 2, with 6 abstentions. The following were present for the vote: Ana Palacio Vallelersundi, chairman; Robert J.E.
