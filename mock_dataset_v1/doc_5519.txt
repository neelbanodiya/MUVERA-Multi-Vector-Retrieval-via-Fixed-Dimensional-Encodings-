Reply (27 February 2012) On 23 January 2012, the Council adopted Decision 2012/35/CFSP which provides, inter alia , for a prohibition on the import, purchase or transport of Iranian crude oil and petroleum products Council Decision 2012/35/CFSP of 23 January 2012 amending Decision 2010/413/CFSP concerning restrictive measures against Iran, OJ L 19, 24.1.2012, p. 22. . This prohibition is without prejudice to contracts concluded before 23 January 2012, provided they are executed no later than 1 July 2012. The decision also stipulates that the Council is to review these measures not later than 1 May 2012, in particular taking due account of the availability and the financial conditions for the supply of crude oil and petroleum products produced in countries other than Iran, with a view to ensuring the continuity of energy supply of Member States. In this regard the Council and the Commission agreed in a Declaration Council and Commission Declaration, OJ C 19, 24.1.2012, p. 6. to revert to the issue of Member States currently importing a large quota of Iranian oil without delay and no later than at the Council meeting in April 2012, with a view to ensuring through all necessary actions the continuity of energy supply, taking into account their national economic situation.
