10 September 2004 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 113 of the Rules of Procedure by Cristiana Muscardini on behalf of the UEN Group on a European council of Islamic communities European Parliament resolution on a European council of Islamic communities The European Parliament , â€“ having regard to Rule 113 of its Rules of Procedure, A. whereas terrorist attacks are on the increase everywhere in the world, B. whereas these barbarous attacks are more and more indiscriminate, targeting defenceless individuals and children in particular, C. whereas it is civilians who are the victims of this brutality, D. whereas terrorism does not even stop at sacrificing child hostages, as recently happened in Ossetia, E. having regard to the underlying causes of these merciless acts of violence, 1. Condemns, in the strongest possible terms, any act of terrorism, for whatever cause, above all acts which target defenceless people and especially children; 2. Condemns any fundamentalism, including religious fundamentalism, that might seek to justify terrorist actions; 3. Condemns any attempt to find sociological explanations or ideological justifications for terrorism, which is ruthlessly intent on killing and is a manifestation of barbarous inhumanity; 4. Calls on the Council, the Commission, the governments of the Member States and all political forces to combat terrorism vigorously, to step up their efforts to overcome divisions between peoples and cultures and to foster mutual understanding; therefore proposes the setting-up of a European council representing Islamic communities in the 25 countries of the Union with the task of devising practical policies for integration, with due regard to the Charter of human rights, the principles of the Community acquis and the laws of EU Member States; 5. Calls on each Member State to encourage the setting-up of formal structures for representation of their Islamic communities.
