Question for written answer E-005730/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Anna Rosbach (ECR) (15 June 2011) Subject: Benefits of increased recycling There are many benefits to recycling, one of which is that it creates jobs. That being so, could the Commission please state: — How many people are currently directly employed in the recycling sector in the EU Member States? — How many people are currently indirectly employed in the recycling sector in the EU Member States? — If the EU Member States doubled the quantity of waste that is recycled, approximately how many jobs would be created as a result?
