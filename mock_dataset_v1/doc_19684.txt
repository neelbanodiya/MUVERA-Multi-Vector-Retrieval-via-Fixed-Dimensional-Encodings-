Answer given by High Representative/Vice‑President Mrs Ashton on behalf of the Commission (30 August 2011) The EU position on the 2011 (second) flotilla aiming to reach Gaza is provided in the joint reply to parliamentary Questions E‑6548/2011 and E‑6617/2011 http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB . In addition to that, EU Member States have exercised the sovereign rights that they have under international law to control maritime traffic within their territorial waters and to give or refuse permission for sailing.
