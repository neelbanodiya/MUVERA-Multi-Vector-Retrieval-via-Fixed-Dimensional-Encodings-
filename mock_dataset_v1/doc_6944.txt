For poultry meat, Commission Regulation (EC) No 543/2008 of 16 June 2008 laying down detailed rules for the application of Council Regulation (EC) No 1234/2007 as regards the marketing standards for poultrymeat OJ L 157, 17.6.2008. provides that, in the case of pre-packed poultrymeat imported from third countries, an indication of the country of origin shall appear on the pre-packaging or on a label attached thereto.
