Joint answer given by Mr Tajani on behalf of the Commission Written questions : E-2939/09 , E-2970/09 , E-2971/09 , E-2972/09 , E-2973/09 , E-2940/09 , E-2941/09 , E-2942/09 , E-2943/09 , E-2944/09 , E-2945/09 , E-2946/09 , E-2947/09 , E-2948/09 , E-2949/09 , E-2950/09 , E-2951/09 , E-2952/09 , E-2953/09 , E-2954/09 , E-2955/09 , E-2956/09 , E-2957/09 , E-2958/09 , E-2959/09 , E-2960/09 , E-2961/09 , E-2962/09 , E-2963/09 , E-2964/09 , E-2965/09 , E-2966/09 , E-2967/09 , E-2968/09 , E-2969/09 (25 May 2009) The Municipalities of Trani, Aci Castello, Acireale, Andria, Avellino, Bari, Barletta, Brindisi, Caltanissetta, Casarano, Cinisi, Copertino, Cosenza, Crotone, Foggia, Francavilla Fontana, Gravina in Puglia, Lecce, Mascalucia, Matera, Mazara del Vallo, Mazzarino, Monreale, Motta Sant'Anastasia, Napoli, Noicattaro, Ostuni, Putignano, Rutigliano, San Severo, Sant'Agata di Militello, Sciacca, Taranto, and Termini Imerese have never been beneficiaries of the Civitas Initiative. The Municipality of Potenza has been beneficiary of the Civitas Initiative. The Commission has carried out the payments established by the relevant grant contract in favour of this Municipality, in conformity with the rules and procedures in force. No fund recovery order proved necessary. The Commission does not disclose the identity of partners involved in proposals which have been submitted to open calls for proposals under the framework programmes for research and technological development. These proposals are submitted for evaluation on a confidential basis and are evaluated by independent external experts. Following the successful negotiation of contracts for proposals, selected as a result of the evaluation, the identity of beneficiaries of grant agreements is made publicly available.
