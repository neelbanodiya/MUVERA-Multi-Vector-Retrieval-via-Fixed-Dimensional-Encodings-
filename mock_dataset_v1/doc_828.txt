WRITTEN QUESTION E-1207/08 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (5 March 2008) Subject: Membership of board of directors of Spanish golf club Could the Commission indicate whether, in its view, a decision by a Spanish golf club to limit membership of its board of directors to either Spanish nationals or Spanish residents is fully compatible with the acquis communitaire? Could it summarise the acquis communitaire on this point?
