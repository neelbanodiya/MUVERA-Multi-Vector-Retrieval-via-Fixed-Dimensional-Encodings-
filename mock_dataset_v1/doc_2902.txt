WRITTEN QUESTION E-3021/06 by Ashley Mote (NI) to the Commission (4 July 2006) Subject: Monitoring the use of EU funds in non-Member States How does the Commission arrange audits, or ensure the proper use of funds made available to non-EU countries? How are checks made on payments, how is compliance with the terms under which funds are released enforced, and how does the Commission ensure it receives accurate information and validated accounts of how the money was used?
