ORAL QUESTION H-0438/02 for Question Time at the part-session in June 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Jonas Sjöstedt to the Commission Subject: Trade Directorate-General's contacts with the European Services Forum The European Services Forum represents Europe's service industry and has contacts with the Commission's Trade Directorate-General and the 133 Committee. It reportedly invited members of the 133 Committee's ad hoc service to a private meeting at the Résidence Palace in Brussels on 28 May 2002 to discuss EU strategy for the GATS 2000 negotiations. The following day, 29 May, the 133 Committee's ad hoc service was to take a decision concerning approval of the draft lists of demands. How far does the Commission's and the 133 Committee's cooperation with the European Services Forum extend? Will the Commission state how many meetings it and the 133 Committee have had with the European Services Forum and what was discussed at those meetings?
