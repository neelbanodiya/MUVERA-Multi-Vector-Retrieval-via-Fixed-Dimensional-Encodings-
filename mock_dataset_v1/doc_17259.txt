Culture, Youth, Education, Media & Sport 2004 - European Year of Education Through Sport Doris PACK (EPP-ED, D) Report on the Council common position for adopting a European Parliament and Council decision establishing the European Year of Education through Sport 2004 (9605/2/2002 – C5-0498/2002 – 2001/0244(COD)) Doc.: A5-0419/2002 Procedure : Codecision procedure (2nd reading) Debate : 19.12.2002 The year 2004 will be the European Year of Education Through Sport. The EU budget for the year will be some €11.5 million. Doris Pack will be recommending the approval of the Council common position without amendment so as to facilitate speedy adoption of the programme in good time for the necessary preparations to be made. All Member States voted in favour of the Common Position except Portugal, who abstained due to lack of an explicit reference to the EURO 2004 football tournament to be held there.
