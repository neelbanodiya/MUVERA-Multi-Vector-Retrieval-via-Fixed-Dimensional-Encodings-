WRITTEN QUESTION E-6502/09 by Hans-Peter Martin (NI) to the Commission (4 January 2010) Subject: EU EULEX mission in Kosovo For a year the EU has been trying, with its EULEX rule-of-law mission, to set up a functioning state of Kosovo. What has the cost of this mission been to date? What are the cost estimates for the future? Exactly what progress was made in the first year, and how does the Commission judge it in relation to the cost? Has the EULEX mission succeeded in stemming the rising tide of corruption in Kosovo?
