Franca Metro Line No 9 has been stuck underground for a year now due to a miscalculation in the EIA with regard to the hardness of the subsoil. Given that the EIA got it wrong with regard to its forecasts about the build-up of water in the subsoil of Barcelona, and the assessment itself warns of the possible consequences for Barcelona’s cultural heritage, is it not the case that the Environmental Impact Assessment should be redone? Should the Commission not ask for further information as Unesco’s technical experts did recently?
