9999/C Q999 E/034 WRITTEN QUESTION P-0770/04 by Seán Ó Neachtain (UEN) to the Commission (01 March 2004) Subject: EU citizenship By 1 May 2004, a number of urgent cases relating to EU citizenship will need to have been clarified. Will the Commission therefore state what are the EU citizenship rights after 1 May 2004 in the following two cases: first, a lady who was born in Russia, moved to Latvia and chose to stay there without citizenship and later married an EU citizen and moved to Ireland where she now has residency rights but requires a visa to travel outside Ireland and, second, Latvian citizens living in Ireland or in any other EU Member State, and their EU citizenship status?
