Question for written answer E-012439/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Liam Aylward (ALDE) , Brian Crowley (ALDE) and Pat the Cope Gallagher (ALDE) (11 January 2012) Subject: Compliance with the WEEE Directive across the Member States Could the Commission clarify which Member States have fully complied with the measures laid down in the WEEE Directive? Could it indicate which Member States have yet to fully implement this directive? What measures can the Commission take to help Member States who have not fully complied with the WEEE Directive to do so within a specific timeframe? Does the Commission have any plans to implement further measures with regard to the management of waste across the Member States?
