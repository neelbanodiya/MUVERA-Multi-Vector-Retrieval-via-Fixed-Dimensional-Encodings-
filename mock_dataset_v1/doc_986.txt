Answer given by Mr Figel' on behalf of the Commission (30 August 2007) The Community funding intended to support, in 2006, innovative cooperation, training and information projects under the Youth programme was awarded on the basis of call for proposals DG EAC 62/05. The list of selected projects does not include any submitted by an Irish promoter. Sixteen projects were selected for Community funding following the call for proposals. A compendium of these projects, available at http://eacea.cec.eu.int/youth/projects/compendia/documents/compendium_2006.pdf , summarises each one.
