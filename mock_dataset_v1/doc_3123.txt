Question for written answer E-003833/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Vladko Todorov Panayotov (ALDE) (18 April 2011) Subject: Oil products — Directive 98/93/EC Can the Commission inform Parliament of the exact legal requirements of Member States and oil companies under Directive 98/93/EC, amending Directive 68/414/EC, imposing an obligation on Member States to maintain minimum stocks of crude oil and/or petroleum products? Are these stocks to be held at Member State level or at the level of each individual company trading or producing and holding oil products? How are companies trading in refinery and/or petroleum products concerned by the provisions of the directive? What is the current stage of implementation of the directive at national level? Has the Commission initiated any infringement proceedings for non-compliance or wrong compliance and, if so, vis-à vis which Member State(s)? Can the Commission provide a list of these Member States and describe the legal steps taken in each case?
