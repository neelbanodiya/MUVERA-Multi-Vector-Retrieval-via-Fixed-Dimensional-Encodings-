Justification This amendment would allow the Agency to provide technical assistance to the Commission in monitoring Member States' implementation of the Directive on enhancing port security. Clearly the Agency's role would be limited to aspects where it had relevant technical expertise; the Commission would be under no obligation to seek assistance and would be unlikely to do so unless it believed the Agency would provide valuable advice.
