WRITTEN QUESTION E-6490/09 by Hans-Peter Martin (NI) to the Commission (4 January 2010) Subject: Opinion on the legality and regularity of the transactions underlying the accounts (‘Economic and financial affairs’) The 2008 annual report of the Court of Auditors states, in its opinion on the legality and regularity of the transactions underlying the accounts (pages 11‑12 of the English-language version), that in the policy group ‘Economic and financial affairs’, and in particular the sixth framework programme for research and technological development (FP6), supervisory and control systems for the policy area ‘Enterprise’ are only partially effective in preventing or detecting and correcting errors. What financial losses did the EU incur in the financial year 2008 as a result of supervisory and control systems for the policy area ‘Enterprise’ being only partially effective? What specific measures is the Commission taking to improve supervisory and control systems for preventing errors in this policy area? What specific measures is the Commission taking to improve supervisory and control systems for detecting errors in this policy area? What specific measures is the Commission taking to improve supervisory and control systems for correcting errors in this policy area?
