Question for written answer E-002525/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 David Campbell Bannerman (EFD) (16 March 2011) Subject: Euro army ‘start-up fund’ During a debate on the common foreign and security policy/common security and defence policy in Brussels at the SEDE Committee in February 2011, there was mention of a ‘start-up fund’ to assist in the activities grouped under these headings. Could the Commission provide more details about the official name for this start-up fund and what activities may be legitimately financed from this fund?
